Introduction

Hello, I'm Luna Howlsyawn. I am a 1 made of 4 that sequenced. On this website I share with you all some information that I have created and changed some over time. If there is one thing I have learned in my life, it is that some things are unavoidable, and some things are not present. Some things are coincidences, and others are not. Contradictions enumerate in so many ways. Questions and questioning are unavoidable and also the only thing that remains after all things are removed, after all the absence is cleared. We are drawn to them like magnets.

Magnet money hell isn't real, it can't hurt you, next question. How? Soap, fat. Salt. Salted property. I do not believe in private property. I am more likely to believe in object-oriented-programming property, individual property, and social property. There. They hide their properties behind variables and operations and cryptographic signals, but it's all just part of laws enforced by compilers and interpreters. You interpret things, like or unlike computers. Physical laws remain. Cryptography applies to information, not unavoidably consistent physics. Sometimes physical symmetries can break though. In order for information to be treated consistently as property, it must be encrypted with passwords, potentially with salted hashes to keep it more secure, or just be unavailable to connection, like being offline. These things are possible and happen, just to be clear. Forces are necessary to maintain known realities and privately or socially organized production.

Rabbits on the moon can only be free if you let them be free. I swept. I slept. You are right there. You are left there. Often. Of. We do things with each other, we say things to each other. For now, this is all that I can say. How can magnetic money Hell be real? It sounds so silly, why would I even need to say it? I often think that hell is a place we make for ourselves and each other by refusing to think and care for each other and ourselves. Questioning is unavoidable and everpresent. Why don't our systems change? Because of game theory? Life is not a game. Erasure is very much like death. Without ranked choice voting, I think a lot of vulnerable people and individuals could be hurt, in the short run and long run.

I hope you enjoy the games and other things I have made. Much of the holoscopes and writing/drawing I do are based on mixes of my unique and common experiences and I hope you find ways to appreciate them in your own ways, without depending too much upon my particular interpretations that brought me to what is here, or my particular outputs.

You are you and that is great. Every moment you spend reading is a moment you absorb. I hope that you do not worry about my particularity or judgement very much, because I honestly just want to share my understandings of truths and my interpretations of empathy with you, as far as I can make it. I probably do not know you, and you can not know me in my entirety, I'm not unique in that respect. Each presence that is present, each now that is given to us, by life itself, by you making you, by you creating you, by each of us, is a present. What you call it is what you call it. What you will call it is what you will call it. We change through spacetime.
